# LinguaCore - Student Mastery Manual 📚

Welcome to **LinguaCore**, the most advanced English learning platform. This manual will guide you through our AI-integrated ecosystem to help you reach native fluency efficiently.

## 🧭 The Learning Ecosystem

### 1. The Power Dashboard
Your central hub for personal growth:
- **Learning Journey Map**: A visual roadmap showing your past, current, and future milestones.
- **Activity Heatmap**: A GitHub-inspired grid tracks your daily study consistency.
- **Leaderboard**: Compete with other students globally to earn the top rank.
- **Digital Certificates**: Upon completing milestones, download your professional B2/C1 certificates with one click.

### 2. Smart Theory & Interactive Practice
Every lesson is designed for maximum retention:
- **Smart Hover Translation**: If you don't understand a word in the theory text, simply **hover your mouse** over it for an instant Spanish translation.
- **AI Speaking Mode**: Practice your pronunciation using our voice recognition system. Hold the microphone button and speak; the AI will score your accuracy in real-time.
- **Audio Feedback**: Use the `[Space]` key to repeat the native pronunciation of any exercise or theory block.

### 3. Study Sprints (Flashcards)
Master new vocabulary using our spaced repetition engine:
- **3D Interactive Cards**: Flip cards to reveal meanings, synonyms, and examples.
- **Quick Hints**: Stuck? Click the "Hint" icon for a partial reveal.
- **Hover-Translate**: Even during sprints, you can hover over the English word to see its translation.

### 4. AI Chat Tutor 🤖
At any time, click the floating bubble in the bottom-right corner:
- Ask grammar questions ("When do I use Present Perfect?").
- Practice open conversation.
- Get instant help if you're stuck on a lesson.

### 5. AI Writing Coach
Refine your formal writing:
- Submit emails, essays, or messages.
- Receive a **Clarity Score** and line-by-line grammatical improvements.

## ⌨️ Professional Keyboard Support
Master the keys to learn 2x faster:
- **[1, 2, 3, 4]**: Select multiple choice options.
- **[Enter]**: Check answer / Continue / Confirm.
- **[Space]**: Replay native audio.
- **[Tab]**: Quick navigation between interactive elements.

## 🌓 Visual Comfort
Customize your environment:
- Toggle between **Light** and **Dark Mode** using the icon in the navigation bar to reduce eye strain during long study sessions.
