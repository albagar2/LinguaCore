# LinguaCore - User Manual 📚

Welcome to **LinguaCore**, your premium English learning platform. This guide will help you understand how to make the most of our features to reach native fluency.

## 🧭 Navigation

### 1. Dashboard (The Command Center)
This is your personal home after logging in. Here you can track:
- **XP (Experience Points):** Earned by completing lessons and exercises.
- **Day Streak:** Keep practicing daily to maintain your streak!
- **Daily Goals:** Checklist of tasks to stay focused.
- **Recommendations:** We suggest the next best lesson for your current level.

### 2. Lessons & Learning
Access the **Lessons** tab to find our curriculum. Each lesson consists of two parts:
- **Theory:** Read and listen to the core concepts. 
- **Practice:** interactive exercises (Multiple Choice, Fill-in-the-blanks).
- **Pro Tip:** Use keyboard shortcuts `1-4` for options and `Enter` to confirm or continue.

### 3. AI Writing Coach ✨
Located in the **AI Coach** tab. 
- Paste any text you've written in English.
- Click "Analyze" and wait for the AI feedback.
- You'll get a clarity score and specific grammatical corrections.

### 4. Vocabulary Glossary
A searchable database of all terms encountered in the lessons.
- Use the **Volume** icon to hear the correct pronunciation.
- Filter by category (Noun, Verb, etc.) or Level.

## ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
To speed up your learning, we've implemented:
- **[1, 2, 3, 4]**: Select multiple choice options.
- **[Enter]**: Check answer / Continue to next step.
- **[Space]**: (Coming soon) Replay audio.

## 📈 Tips for Success
1. **Consistency is Key:** Practicing 10 minutes every day is better than 2 hours once a week. Your **Streak** counter will help you stay motivated.
2. **Listen and Repeat:** Use the speaker icons in the Glossary and Theory sections to practice your pronunciation.
3. **Use the AI Coach:** Every time you write an email or a message in English, check it in the Writing Coach first to learn from your mistakes.
